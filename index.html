our services

Medical Consultation

We offer free online consultation with experienced Indian medical professionals who will provide an expert opinion on your medical condition.

Hospital & Doctor Selection

We work with leading hospitals and specialized doctors across India to provide a wide range of medical services, including cancer treatment, cardiac care, neurosurgery, and much more.

Travel and Accommodation

Our team will help you to arrange flights, visas, and comfortable accommodation near the hospital, making your medical trip to India as easy as possible.

Personalized Care

Our dedicated team will provide you with personalized care and support throughout your medical journey. We will assist you with translation, interpretation, and provide you with a local sim card to stay in touch with your loved ones.

After-Care

We understand the importance of proper follow-up care and support after medical treatment. Our team will ensure that you receive proper after-care services, including regular check-ups and follow-up consultations.
